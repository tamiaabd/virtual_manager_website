About the App

Built for Businesses That Keep It Simple

Virtual Manager is a business operations app designed for small shops, traders, and retail businesses. Instead of scattered notebooks and spreadsheets, you get one organized system to handle everything — from recording a sale to checking your monthly report.

The app helps reduce the burden of manual record keeping, keeps your business data organized, and gives you quick visibility into how your business is performing — all without needing any accounting expertise.
